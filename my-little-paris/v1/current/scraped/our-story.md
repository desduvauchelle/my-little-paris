# Our Story

During a trip to Taiwan, we went to a family restaurant that had a playground next to the dining area. For the first time as parents, we were able to sit down and eat together—without having one of us take care of our kid while the other tries to eat quickly—because our kid was having so much fun in the playground! It was such a wonderful experience, that we wanted to create a similar concept so that other parents could also enjoy dining out with their children…

We want to open a place where parents can relax and take a break… while children can have fun in a safe environment… where parents don't have to worry about tidying the toys after play time… where parents can have more food and drink choices on the menu… where parents can come with their friends and have a conversation while enjoying a glass of wine or some beer… And so a new business was born!

We want to let everyone have a good and unforgettable time at our place!

---

## My Little Paris (the name)

We are originally from Paris and moved to Los Angeles a few years ago… Through our café and play, we want to share some French "art de vivre" experience with you!

Our place has been created to welcome parents with their children. We know that every parents always have a cute nickname for their little ones—such as my little cutie, my little princess, my little munchkin, etc…

And this is how we named our place My Little Paris…
