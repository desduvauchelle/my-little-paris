## A unique café… Take a break… Enjoy your time…

My Little Paris is a family friendly café with an indoor play space where parents can enjoy a delicious meal and children can play in a safe area. The restaurant features healthy and nutritious meals as well as French pastries and desserts. The play space provides a safe, clean and stimulating environment for active children to play in and explore.

We understand that before coming, people usually plan ahead and/or promise their kids to go play in the playground. And if the place is closed or full when you arrive, there's nothing worst than to see the disappointment in the eyes of your little ones! So please, make a reservation so we can make sure to have a seat for you.

## Cleaning & Sanitizing

We clean and sanitize regularly throughout the day and may temporarily close the play space for a few minutes to tidy up. We apologize for the inconvenience when we have to temporarily close the play space during your Eat & Play session.

On weekends, we may close between two sessions to clean and sanitize. So be sure to check our hours and to arrive on time at your Eat & Play session because we may or may not be able to extend it, should you arrive late.
