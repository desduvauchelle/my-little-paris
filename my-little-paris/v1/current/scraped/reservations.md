# Reservations

Each reservation slot can accommodate up to 6 people maximum (including adults and children).

When you make a reservation, we hold a table for your group and may block other slots. In some cases, we may also need to turn away walk-in customers if we are fully booked. We kindly ask that you honor your reservation time. Due to a high number of no-shows, we require a credit card on file for reservations of more than 6 people.

For groups larger than 6 people, please email us at hello@my-little-paris.com with the following details:

- Your full name
- Number of adults and children
- Desired reservation date and time

A credit card on file will be required to secure your booking and help prevent last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

We can accommodate Eat & Play group reservations for up to 12 people. For groups larger than 12, you will have to book our private room. [sic — original text: "you will have to book our private room must be booked."]

If you are interested in hosting a party, please check out our party packages for full details. → /party

Thank you for your understanding and support!
